1) spell (verb)
  - Variations: spelled, spelling
  - Transitive verb:
      - To name the letters of in order
      - To write or print the letters of in a particular way
      - To make up (a word)
        - Ex: What word do these letters spell?
      - Write sense 1b
        - Ex: Catnip is spelled as one word.
      - To add up to : mean
        - Ex: crop failure was likely to spell stark famine—Stringfellow Barr
      - To find out by study : come to understand
        - Ex: it requires some pains to spell out those decorations—F. J. Mather
      - To read slowly and with difficulty
